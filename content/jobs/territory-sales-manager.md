## About the Role

Tiger BioSciences is expanding our commercial team and seeking driven Territory Sales Managers to grow our regenerative medicine portfolio across multiple therapeutic areas. This role offers the unique opportunity to represent truly innovative products that improve patient outcomes while building a lucrative career with a rapidly growing leader in the tissue-based therapeutics space.

As a Territory Sales Manager, you'll develop and execute sales strategies within your assigned geography, building relationships with wound care centers, plastic surgeons, orthopedic surgeons, and hospital systems. You'll be the face of Tiger BioSciences in your market, educating healthcare providers on the clinical benefits of our advanced biomaterial solutions and driving product adoption.

## Key Responsibilities

- Achieve and exceed quarterly and annual sales targets within assigned territory
- Develop and execute strategic territory business plans to maximize market penetration
- Build and maintain relationships with key opinion leaders, surgeons, and wound care specialists
- Conduct product evaluations and in-service training for clinical staff
- Support cases in OR and wound care settings to ensure successful product implementation
- Manage distributor relationships and coordinate with regional distribution partners
- Maintain accurate CRM records and provide regular sales forecasts
- Attend and present at local medical conferences and symposia
- Stay current on competitive landscape and market dynamics
- Collaborate with marketing and medical affairs teams on educational initiatives

## Required Qualifications

- Bachelor's degree in life sciences, business, or related field
- Minimum 3 years of successful medical device or biologics sales experience
- Proven track record of exceeding sales quotas and driving territory growth
- Strong understanding of hospital purchasing processes and GPO contracting
- Excellent presentation and communication skills
- Ability to understand and articulate clinical data and product differentiation
- Self-motivated with strong organizational and time-management skills
- Willingness to travel extensively within territory (60-70%)
- Valid driver's license and clean driving record

## Preferred Qualifications

- Experience selling tissue-based products, skin substitutes, or advanced wound care
- Existing relationships with plastic surgeons, wound care specialists, or orthopedic surgeons
- Knowledge of reimbursement landscape for biologics and tissue-based products
- Experience with value analysis committees and formulary management
- Clinical background (RN, NP, or PA) is a plus

## What We Offer

- Company vehicle or car allowance
- Comprehensive benefits package (medical, dental, vision, 401k match)
