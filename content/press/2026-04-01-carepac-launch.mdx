**CONSHOHOCKEN, Pa., April 1, 2026** /PRNewswire/ -- Tiger BioSciences announced the launch of carePAC™, a line of pre-configured wound care convenience kits designed to simplify dressing changes and improve access to essential wound care supplies for patients managing wounds at home.

carePAC™ kits contain carefully selected wound care components packaged together to support consistent, patient-friendly dressing changes. By providing the necessary materials in a single, easy-to-use format, carePAC™ aims to reduce complexity for patients and caregivers while supporting adherence to provider-directed wound care protocols.

The launch represents a continued expansion of Tiger BioSciences' commitment to improving patient access to high-quality wound care solutions both in the clinic and at home.

Each carePAC™ kit is designed to provide the supplies needed for routine dressing changes in a sealed, organized pouch format. The kits are intended to support both patients and caregivers by reducing the need to source multiple individual products while promoting consistent wound care practices.

"Wound care does not stop when a patient leaves the clinic. With carePAC™, we bridge the gap between clinical and home settings by delivering convenient kits that simplify dressing changes," stated Garrett Grinsfelder, President of Tiger Wound Care.

"carePAC™ was developed with providers and patients in mind. Pre-configured components streamline care delivery, reduce patient confusion, and support improved wound management outcomes," said Oliver Burckhardt, Co-CEO of Tiger BioSciences.

**About Tiger BioSciences**

Tiger BioSciences, headquartered in Conshohocken, PA, is a leader in regenerative medicine specializing in repair and regeneration of human tissue compromised by disease, trauma, injury, and aging. The vertically integrated tissue engineering company specializes in recovery, processing, development, research, and distribution of cellular, acellular, and matrix-like products and aesthetic technologies.
