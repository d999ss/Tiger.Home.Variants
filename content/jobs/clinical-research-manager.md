## About the Role

We are seeking an experienced Clinical Research Manager to oversee clinical trials and generate robust clinical evidence for our regenerative medicine product portfolio. This role is critical in advancing the scientific foundation of our tissue-based therapies and ensuring that healthcare providers have the data they need to make informed treatment decisions.

You will manage all aspects of clinical study execution, from protocol development through publication, working closely with principal investigators at leading academic medical centers. This position requires both strategic thinking and hands-on execution, as you'll be responsible for building Tiger BioSciences' clinical evidence base in wound care, plastic surgery, and orthopedic applications.

## Key Responsibilities

- Design and execute clinical trials for tissue-based products (prospective studies, registries, RWE)
- Develop clinical protocols, informed consent documents, and case report forms
- Manage relationships with clinical investigators, IRBs, and contract research organizations
- Oversee study timelines, budgets, and regulatory compliance (ICH-GCP, 21 CFR Part 812)
- Analyze clinical data and prepare manuscripts for peer-reviewed publication
- Support regulatory submissions with clinical sections of 510(k) and investigational device applications
- Collaborate with medical affairs on investigator-initiated studies
- Present clinical data at medical conferences and to internal stakeholders
- Mentor clinical research coordinators and associates

## Required Qualifications

- Advanced degree (MS, PhD, PharmD, or MD) in life sciences or clinical discipline
- Minimum 7 years of clinical research experience in medical devices, biologics, or regenerative medicine
- Proven track record of managing multi-center clinical trials from start to finish
- Strong knowledge of FDA regulations governing clinical investigations
- Experience with clinical trial management systems and electronic data capture platforms
- Excellent project management skills with ability to manage multiple concurrent studies
- Strong statistical knowledge and experience working with biostatisticians
- Outstanding written communication skills for protocol development and publication

## Preferred Qualifications

- Experience with tissue-based products or HCT/Ps (Human Cells, Tissues, and Cellular and Tissue-based Products)
- Clinical background in wound care, plastic surgery, or orthopedics
- Published author in peer-reviewed medical journals
- Experience with real-world evidence studies and patient registries
- Knowledge of health economics and outcomes research methodologies
- Certification in clinical research (CCRC, CCRA, or equivalent)
