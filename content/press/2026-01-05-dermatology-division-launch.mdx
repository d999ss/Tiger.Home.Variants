**CONSHOHOCKEN, Pa., Jan. 5, 2026** /PRNewswire/ -- Tiger BioSciences officially launched its Dermatology Division, expanding the company's offerings in wound care and aesthetic solutions. The new division provides dermatologists with access to placental-derived wound care products, HealPack surgical dressings, and cosmetic offerings.

The division operates through a dedicated distribution channel with specialized support for dermatology practices.

"Dermatologists are uniquely positioned at the intersection of wound and aesthetic treatments," stated Scott Madden, Co-CEO of Tiger BioSciences.

Oliver Burckhardt, Co-CEO, emphasized the company's focus: "Our goal is to provide dermatologists with best-in-class aesthetic innovations backed by scientific and clinical data."

The launch combines Tiger BioSciences' existing wound care technologies with aesthetic solutions, creating a comprehensive offering for dermatologists managing both clinical wounds and cosmetic treatments. The division includes expanded field support and access to scientific resources.

**About Tiger BioSciences**

Tiger BioSciences, headquartered in Conshohocken, PA, specializes in cellular and tissue technology, processing innovations, and aesthetic devices across multiple medical applications.
